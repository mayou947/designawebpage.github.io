About

Introduction

Nowadays, there is a large number of websites and pages on the Internet, including both good websites and poorly designed websites. Also, most businesses in the world essentially develop and provide services through their websites. There is no doubt that it is essential - at least it would be better - to use web technology; typically making a pleasing website.

Although it is rarely said that there is a perfect website that has totally unique and correct content, wonderful design, and always perfectly working functionalities, web developers try to accomplish the ideal goal, which helps them to make a better website.

Purpose

In this website, elements that compose a good website will be introduced and explained, and four sites are to be reviewed based on those criteria. This leads to a better understanding of developing a good website, especially from a design perspective.
